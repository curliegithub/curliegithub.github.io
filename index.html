About Me!

Get to Know Me

Hey there! My name is Greta Holland, I go under the name Curlie (or variants of) on most of my social media. I am a Maltese traditional and digital artist and beginner animator. I mostly draw characters from different media, as well as my own characters. I am studying Game Art and Visual Design. As for experience, I have sold my art at Comic-Con 2018, and started seriously uploading animations on Youtube, Instagram and Twitter in July 2021. I have also taken part in a for-profit zine. The traditional medium I use most are acrylics, and the software I use for digital art is currently Procreate.
